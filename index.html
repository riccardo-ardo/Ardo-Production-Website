About

One studio.
One lead.
No handoffs.

Ardo Productions is the studio of Riccardo Montali — designer, developer, and direct creative lead across every discipline. When you work with Ardo, you work with the person building it.

No account managers diluting the brief. No junior hands executing the concept. The vision you approve is the vision that gets built — from initial renders to the final installed stand or deployed app.

The studio spans physical and digital outcomes: brand activations and expo environments that perform on the floor, alongside Wix platforms and custom applications that perform in the browser.

Built for brands that need to be remembered — not just seen.
